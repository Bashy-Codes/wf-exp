# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
WorldFriends is a cross-cultural social networking mobile application built with React Native and Expo. It connects people from different countries and cultures, enabling meaningful friendships through various communication channels including posts, letters, conversations, and community discussions.

## Key Features and Capabilities

### Social Networking
- **Feed System**: Create and share  posts with images, reactions, and comments
- **Collections**: Organize and curate posts into themed collections
- **User Discovery**: Find and connect with people based on filters (age, gender, country, languages)
- **Friendships**: Send and manage friend requests, maintain friend lists

### Communication Channels
- **Conversations**: Real-time one-on-one messaging with friends
- **Letters**: Compose and send thoughtful letters with scheduling
- **Communities**: Create and join interest-based communities
- **Discussions**: Participate in threaded discussions within communities

### Cultural Exchange
- **Multi-language Support**: 10 languages (English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Turkish, Chinese)
- **Translation Features**: Built-in translation capabilities for cross-language communication
- **Language Learning**: Flashcard system and quiz functionality for language study
- **Cultural Guidelines**: Community guidelines promoting respectful cross-cultural interaction

### Engagement Features
- **Virtual Store**: Purchase and gift virtual items to friends
- **Inventory System**: Track owned products, sent gifts, and received gifts
- **Notifications**: Push notifications for messages, friend requests, and community activity
- **Profile Customization**: Detailed profiles with photos, collections, and personal information

### Moderation & Safety
- **Content Moderation**: Report system for inappropriate content
- **Community Guidelines**: Clear rules for respectful interaction
- **Privacy Controls**: User profile visibility and interaction settings

## Target Users and Use Cases

### Primary Users
- **Cultural Enthusiasts**: Individuals interested in learning about different cultures
- **Language Learners**: People wanting to practice languages with native speakers
- **International Students**: Students seeking connections with peers from other countries
- **Travel Enthusiasts**: People planning to travel or interested in global friendships
- **Expats**: Individuals living abroad looking to connect with locals or fellow expats

### Key Use Cases
1. **Cultural Learning**: Engage in discussions about customs, traditions, and daily life
2. **Pen Pal Experience**: Exchange thoughtful letters with international friends
3. **Community Building**: Join communities based on shared interests or cultural topics
4. **Language Exchange**: Connect with native speakers for language practice
5. **Travel Preparation**: Connect with locals before visiting a new country
6. **Long-term Friendships**: Build meaningful cross-cultural relationships over time

## Value Proposition
WorldFriends bridges geographical and cultural boundaries by providing a safe, engaging platform for authentic international friendships. Unlike generic social networks, it's specifically designed for cross-cultural exchange with features like scheduled letters, translation tools, and language learning resources that facilitate meaningful connections across language barriers.
